Cynthia Buchira

Advisory board member

Cynthia Buchira is a young visionary HIV prevention Ambassador and advocate, recently appointed as a member of the positive young women voices Advisory Group. My passion as a volunteer at PYWV comes from a life long obsession to end HIV stigma and new infections and also end GBV among Adolescent girls and young women. The simple act of shaping the community to make something functional or beautiful brings me endless satisfaction.
