Community engagement




We hold monthly community outreaches and dialogues on violence against women and girls in their diversity . Our outreaches are an avenue for us to listen, inform and respond to the needs of community members within the five phases in Dandora , all while creating linkages with local authorities on SGBV response, raising awareness on our programmes and offering referrals to SRH services for women and girls. Community Dialogues are structured forums derived from the outreaches, bringing together select members from the community to discuss peaceful means of solving conflicts, with a focus on preventing violence against women and girls in homes and within intimate partner relationships
