Gender Rights Promotion




The LBQ+ Forum comprises of Lesbians, Bisexuals and Queer persons living with and affected by HIV. The forum came into existence to address the challenges the LBQ+ Women are facing such as Sexual Violence, Disownment, Stigma and discrimination based on Sexual Orientations and Expressions and Healthcare.

We Hold Monthly Physical Forums and Bi-Weekly Online Forums that enable the dissemination of LBQ+ tailored Sexual and reproductive health(SRH) Information, Creation of linkages with service providing organizations that offer SRH services, Creation of a safe space for LBQ+ womxn to meet and Ensure security and protection of the human rights of Lesbian, Bisexual and Queer womxn.
