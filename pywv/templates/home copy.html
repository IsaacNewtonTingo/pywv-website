Who are we?

Positive Young Women Voices (PYWV) is a community based organization which started as a self-help group in the year 2014 but later registered as a community based organization in the year 2017. The idea of group formation was derived when these advocates noticed there were major challenges facing young women and girls living with and at risk of HIV within the Nairobi slums of Dandora.The major challenges include teenage pregnancy, poverty, gender based violence, access to friendly health facility and information.Armed with experience they chose to fill the gaps by offering mentorship, provide linkages and referrals, create awareness and offer information.

Ending violence against women and girls

Behind every woman and girl there is an untold story and in most case is a sad story. Women and girls go through various challenges in life; gender based violence being the most rampant. This is due to the patriarchal system and cultural factors that deny women power to decide on their human rights.

LBQ support forums

PYWV LBQ Support forum comprises of Lesbians, Bisexuals and Queer persons living with and affected by HIV from our community Dandora.

Our activities

Empowerment

The empowerment program will be building the capacities of young women and girls affected and living with HIV to get the best care, treatment and support and to improve their lives with the right information as well as economic growth for sustainable livelihoods.

Prevention

PYWV prevention program will major on prevention with positive program where the young women who are influencers will act as mentors to the younger ones through psycho social support, peer education, facilitation of referrals for treatment ,screening and management of other non-communicable diseases and awareness creation.
