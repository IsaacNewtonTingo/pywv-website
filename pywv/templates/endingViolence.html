Prevention And Ending Violence Against Women And Girls




We live in a society where women and girls are not safe anywhere and if we are to stand in solidarity, we have to pledge to free women and girls from the shackles of violence. Under the project, "Preventing and Ending Violence against women and Girls" . We engage young women in their diversity within the five phases in Dandora including teenage mothers, survivors of gender based violence and those most affected through trainings conducted bi-montly discussing root causes of violence, principles of gender and the impact it creates in women, girls and community at large from a range of perspectives.

In efforts of preventing and ending violence against women and girls, we use a gender transformative intervention , involving young men and understanding the role they play as influencers and allies. We enhance victim/survivor centered response to violence against women and girls by creating linkages with local authorities as well as referrals to the organizations and shelters responding to gender based violence .

We trend on social media with the #ItEndsWithUs to start conversations, and urge the society to believe the survivors, we empower and stand against violence against women and girls.
