LBQ Support Forums

PYWV LBQ Support forum comprises of Lesbians, Bisexuals and Queer persons living with and affected by HIV from our community Dandora. The support forum came into existence because of the challenges the LBQ Womxn are facing such as:

  • Sexual Violence
  • Disownment
  • Stigma and discrimination based on Sexual Orientations and Expressions
This has led to significant health and social-economic negative outcomes on this group and this saw the beginning of this safe space created. It started on a WhatsApp group and still has weekly conversations on the group on key issues that are important to LBQ womxn. The monthly support forums have enabled:
  • The dissemination of LBQ tailored Sexual and reproductive health(SRH) Information to its members.
  • Creation of linkages with services providing organizations that offer SRH services to our members.
  • Creation of a safe space for LBQ womxn to meet monthly.
  • Ensure security and protection of the human rights of Lesbian, Bisexual and Queer (LBQ) women.
